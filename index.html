WHAT?

D'Kalp is a Design competition but unlike the usual ones, it is focused on learning rather than just competing. The reason behind the emphasis on learning is that students from non-design backgrounds can also take part along with the design students, as the design thinking is currently the booming word in the industry and is a key skill for all.This kind of learning competition can make participants think in an innovative direction and can pursue their dreams of starting businesses or startups.

WHO?

We are not limiting this for just university/institute students but also encouraging school students, mainly those who have an interest in the design field (i.e., a Design Aspirant).

This kind of event will help them become aware of design education through the competition and also help them to build their portfolio!

Eligibility criteria

Participants can participate individually or in a team of 2 people only.

Level 1 - School students studying in class 9th or above. (diploma students are also eligible). Students who took a year drop after class 12, can also participate in this category. Now Students pursuing Under Graduation from any discipline are eligible for this category.

Level 2 - Students pursuing Under Graduation from any discipline are eligible for this category. Students who have graduated in/after 2019 and took a drop for PG preparation can participate in this category. One must not be a post graduate student. Entries might get disqualified at any time if found.

Level 3 - Students pursuing Post Graduation from any discipline are eligible for this category. People who have upto 3 years of industry experience (after graduating from bachelors in 2017, 2018 or 2019 and willing to pursue their PG in Design) can also participate in this level.
